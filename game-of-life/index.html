--- permalink: /game-of-life --- Game Of Life
Init
  • Spawn Glider
  • Spawn Pulsar
  • Spawn r-Pentomino
  • Spawn Heavy Weight Spaceship
  • Spawn Gosper's Glider Gun
Source code (on GitHub) How to play
  • Left Click: set cell to black (living cell)
  • Right Click: set cell to white (dead cell)
  • Once the game is running, you can only edit the grid by stopping the game
  • Use your mouse wheel to zoom (grid is fixed to center)
 Rules
  • Living cells with 2 or 3 neighbours survive, if the amount of neighbours is smaller than 2 or greater than 3 the cell will die (will become white)
  • Dead cells with exactly 3 neighbours spawn (become black)
  • One cell has 8 neighbours (a square around the cell)
