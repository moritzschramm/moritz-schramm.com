Projects

Typos

Typos is an online trainer with which you can learn the touch typing system. The app loads the training content and displays a virtual keyboard that shows which keys to press, while more experienced users can also hide the keyboard. The app calculates the speed and the accuracy of the user in the background. In the full, non-static version, users can choose between lections and training sessions, commonly misspelled words will be asked more frequently in future sessions and users can create own lections and practice with them as well.

EasyNotify

EasyNotify is a note reminder app for android devices. The purpose of the app is to organize notes as easily as possible. You can add so called 'Notifys' to lists. The app will display a permanent notification of each list in the notification bar, so that you will always see your notes when you check your notifications. There is also a reminder service with which you can create a reminder with a time at, for example, 1 pm every day. The app will then send you a notification at that point of time. Of course you can also choose the interval of recurring reminders. Currently, the app has over 50,000 downloads and over 1,500 ratings with an average rating of 4.5 out of 5 stars.

Senioren Service Neustadt

The website of Senioren-Service-Neustadt e.V. was completely remade. Both the design, as well as the hosting, email and domain providers have been changed. At first, the new website was developed using Jekyll, and the repository was uploaded to a server. Next, the email provider has been changed and the requied DNS records were added. To avoid downtime, the records of the nameservers were changed at the very end and the new site was made public.
